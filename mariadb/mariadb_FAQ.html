Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • General Questions about Cloud Hosting for MariaDB™

      01. What service do you provide?

    • What we provide is a fully managed hosting service for MariaDB™. Our hosting environment is built on our self-developed cloud application management platform.

      02. What's your cloud hosting platform for MariaDB™?

    • It is our quick evolving hosting solution for MariaDB™. It takes advantage of container and Kubernetes, which is the most popular and dominant container-orchestration system.

      03. What does the fully managed hosting service mean?

    • Fully managed hosting means that all MariaDB™ on our platform are completely managed by us. Our maintenance work includes but not limited to the application deployment, migration, backup, security and upgrade, leaving you time and energy to stay focused on the application development.

      04. What functions does your control panel provide?

    • Aside from general operations to manage your hosting, our control panel supports administrative functions for MariaDB™, including FTPS server, monitoring metrics, and Shell / SSH.

      05. How do you provide technical support?

    • We provide technical support 24/7 via email. Sales department is available 24/7 via live chat and email. Billing department can be approached Monday - Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m via email.

      06. What is the philosophy behind your managed hosting service for MariaDB™?

    • Our philosophy is to provide managed hosting services for MariaDB™ with affordable prices to small and medium-sized businesses.

      07. How many data centers do you have?

    • Currently, we have four data centers located in Dallas, Bend, Charlotte, and Denver, allowing clients to deploy applications as close to their target audience as possible.

  • Technical Questions about Cloud Hosting for MariaDB™

      01. What MariaDB™ version do you support?

    • Currently, we support MariaDB™ 10.5 only.

      02. Does your managed hosting for MariaDB™ support SSL connection?

    • Yes. The SSL connection is enabled by default for each newly created MariaDB™ on our cloud hosting platform for MariaDB™.

      03. Do you do backups for my online MariaDB™ database?

    • Yes. Our Kubernetes platform takes backups regularly. The backup frequency varies by hosting plan. Please note that we implement weekly backup for free-trial applications.

      04. Can I take backups of my MariaDB™ in your managed hosting for MariaDB™?

    • Yes. You can back up your MariaDB™ via our hosting control panel and download them.

      05. Do you have access limits to my MariaDB™ in the managed hosting service for MariaDB™?

    • No. We do not set limits on your access to MariaDB™.

      06. How can I manage my MariaDB™ databases and users?

    • You can manage users in the control panel in our cloud hosting for MariaDB™. For detailed steps, please refer to the documentation.

      07. Is your cloud hosting for MariaDB™ shared?

    • No. Our fully managed hosting for MariaDB™ on Kubernetes is based on standalone MariaDB™ instance.

      08. What cloud providers and regions do you support in your cloud hosting for MariaDB™?

    • We currently support the private cloud from our four data centers in Dallas, Bend, Charlotte, and Denver. We are building more data centers and will support most of the popular public cloud providers in more regions in the near future.

      09. What MariaDB™ storage engine do you support?

    • We currently support InnoDB in our managed hosting for MariaDB™.

      10. How can I migrate my MariaDB™ database onto your platform?

    • We support the standard MariaDB™ tool to restore your local backup onto our Kubernetes-based platform.

      11. Can I monitor the resource usage on managed hosting plans for MariaDB™?

    • Yes. Our control panel supports real-time resource usage monitoring. Please refer to this guide article on how you can do it.

      12. Do you support MariaDB™ Cluster?

    • No. We support single-instance MariaDB™ deployment.

      13. What is the default maximum number of connections for online MariaDB™?

    • The maximum connections are 1000.

      14. Do you provide root user for online MariaDB™?

    • No, we do not provide a root user by default. However, clients can create super users in our client panel.

  • Sales Questions about Cloud Hosting for MariaDB™

      01. Can I try your cloud hosting plans before payment?

    • Yes. For each account, permitted concurrent free-trial deployments are up to 3.

      02. What hosting plan should I start with?

    • If you are not sure what plan you should start with, you can choose a primitive plan and upgrade to a higher level when necessary. The hosting plan upgrade for MariaDB™ is very smooth here. There is almost no downtime during the process.

      03. How long does it take to set up my MariaDB™ online?

    • The deployment on our platform can be a matter of minutes, even seconds.

      04. Do you have a download file and IP that I can test the network speed?

    • Yes. You can test our network speed at the Data Centers page.

      05. Do you offer any network and server uptime guarantee?

    • Yes. We offer a 99.9% uptime guarantee for our network and all servers.

      06. What is difference between free trial services and paid services?

    • In the free-trial period, you will be able to enjoy our paid services for free.

      07. How can I continue my service after the free trial?

    • If you decide to continue using the service, you need to prepay for the hosting service before the trial is over. We will send you an invoice after you complete your billing information so that you can make the payment upfront. If you need help on this, please refer to the link.

      08. How can I get my free trial?

    • To start a free trial, please go to the Pricing page and click the 'Free Trial' button at the end of your interested plan.

      09. Can I end my trial and upgrade to a paid subscription?

    • Of course. You can upgrade to the paid subscription at any time during the free trial. Thank you.
